Benefits
• FoliStar Booster produces greater root mass and green
   leaf area.
• FoliStar Booster gives improved flower/fruit set and
   higher tolerance to environmental stress.
• It is used in the initial Stage Sprays for Green Growth.
• It also develops tolerance towards abiotic stress.
