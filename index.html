About Foliva

A modern agribusiness brand built around science, field relevance and responsible growth.

Welcome to Foliva, your trusted partner in sustainable agriculture. Foliva is committed to improving the way fertilizers are produced and used to promote healthier ecosystems, enhance crop yields and support long-term agricultural productivity.

Founded on the principles of innovation, sustainability and environmental stewardship, Foliva offers fertilizers tailored to diverse farming needs through foliar nutrition, water soluble fertilizers, granular soil conditioners and crop-specific fertilizer kits.
